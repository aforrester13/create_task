    GPA Calculator

    GPA, or grade-point average, is one of the most important aspects of student life. It defines your overall academic ability to learn, comprehend, and reproduce the information when tested upon it. However, many do not know how it is actually calculated. The formula for your grade- point average is (GRADES * CREDITS) / CREDITS. It takes the grade you earned for a class, multiplies it with the number of credits or credit hours received for that specific class, and does this for each class you partake in. Then it adds these values together and divides it by the total amount of credits or credit hours to get your GPA value. What we have done is made it much simpler than doing all of this math yourself and created a calculator that does it for you! Simply input your letter grades, the number of credit hours spent on each class, and press CALCULATE, and there's your GPA!

    QPA Calculator

    QPA, or quality-point average, is very similar to the way GPA is calculator, but is specific to number grades instead of letter grades. It's very uncommon in most schools, however, still provides some importance if a students wants to know their exact number grade. It is calculated by the formula (GRADES * CREDITS) / CREDITS as well, however, the grades are numbers, not letters, and hours are actually equal to the credits received from taking a class or course. Multiply the individual grade by the credit cost of the class, add them all up and divide by the total number of hours to get your result. Our calculator does all of this tedious math for you, letting you acquire your number grade in an instant

    Measurement Converter

    As we all know, the world uses two different types of measurement system; the Metric and the U.S. Standard system. The Metric system uses simple multiples of ten to describe length, weight, and volume, while the U.S. standard system does not have a specific rule set for its measurements. For this converter, we will be focusing on the length and measurement branch of the Metric V.S. Standard System. In this converter, you will be able to change between centimeters to inches, to miles, kilometers to meters, and so on.
